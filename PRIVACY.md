## 2. Overview

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data when you use the SmarterGerman Conversation Practice Tool ("the Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

## 3. Data We Collect

### 3.1 Account Information
- Email address (from course platform authentication)
- Course enrollment status

### 3.2 Conversation Data
- **Voice recordings:** Audio captured during conversation sessions
- **Transcriptions:** Text transcriptions of your speech and AI responses
- **Session metadata:** Timestamps, session duration, language settings

### 3.3 Technical Data
- IP address (for security and rate limiting)
- Browser type and version
- Device information

## 4. How We Use Your Data

We process your data for the following purposes:

| Purpose | Legal Basis (GDPR) |
|---------|-------------------|
| Provide conversation practice | Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Authenticate users | Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)) |
| Improve AI responses | Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) |
| Prevent abuse/fraud | Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) |
## 5. AI Service Provider

Your voice data is processed by a third-party AI provider to generate conversational responses.

### Google Gemini

- **Company:** Google LLC
- **Data location:** United States / European Union
- **Legal framework:** EU-US Data Privacy Framework, Google Cloud Data Processing Addendum
- **Privacy policy:** https://policies.google.com/privacy

Google processes your voice data to generate AI responses. Data may be processed in the US under the EU-US Data Privacy Framework, which provides GDPR-equivalent protections.

## 6. Data Retention

| Data Type | Retention Period |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Voice recordings | Not stored (streamed directly to AI) |
| Session transcriptions | 24 hours |
| Usage statistics | 30 days |
| Account data | Duration of course enrollment |
| Consent records | 3 years (legal requirement) |

## 7. Data Sharing

We share your data with:

- **AI provider** (Google) - to generate conversation responses
- **Course platforms** (Teachable/LifterLMS) - to verify enrollment
- **Cloud infrastructure** (Google Cloud Run) - to host the service

We do not sell your personal data.

## 8. Your Rights (GDPR Articles 15-22)

As an EU resident, you have the right to:

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Access** (Art. 15) | Request a copy of your personal data |
| **Rectification** (Art. 16) | Correct inaccurate data |
| **Erasure** (Art. 17) | Request deletion of your data |
| **Restriction** (Art. 18) | Limit how we process your data |
| **Portability** (Art. 20) | Receive your data in a portable format |
| **Object** (Art. 21) | Object to processing based on legitimate interest |
| **Withdraw consent** (Art. 7) | Withdraw consent for data processing |

To exercise these rights, contact privacy@smartergerman.com.

## 9. Security Measures

We implement the following security measures:

- TLS encryption for all data in transit
- WebSocket secure connections (WSS)
- Rate limiting to prevent abuse
- Session tokens with short expiry
- No permanent storage of voice recordings

## 10. Children's Privacy

This service is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

## 11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated via the course platform or email.
